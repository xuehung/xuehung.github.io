Project

Video Streaming Interception via RDP, 2013
When playing streaming videos via remote desktop protocol (RDP), encoded frames will be transmitted to the client and be decoded. This approach greatly reduce the traffic between the RDP server and client. However, when the bandwidth become small and is not enough for transmitted packets, users may have poor experience. Therefore, I developed a middleware which can drop less important packets according to the available bandwidth, and thus reduce the traffic between both end-hosts. The video shows that our solution (left side) is smoother than that without our proposed middleware.
Preference-Aware Opportunistic Content Dissemination for Cellular Traffic Offloading, 2013
As mobile devices become more ubiquitous, the amount of cellular traffic for multimedia content dissemination grows explosively. Therefore, data dissemination through proximity-based opportunistic communications has attracted a great amount of attention . Thus, I proposed an algorithm to select a set of users with initial content objects to offload 3G traffic with proximity-based communication in a mobile social network in consideration of both social structures and heterogeneous preferences. The trace-driven simulation shows about 49.3% improvement in average utility with our proposed source selection algorithm.
Smart Home Box, Undergraduate Researh Project, 2011
There are many sensors installed at the future smart home environment. In order to provide a platform for sensors to work together and assist users install devices easily, I implemented a gateway called Smart Home Box by imploying Apache ActiveMQ as the communication protocol and JSON as the format for data exchange. There are two core functions: device discovery and service composition. Device discovery enables the gateway to react to addition and removal of sensors. It automatically fetches the newly added sensor's manifest file which describes its capabilities so that users can operate sensors without the prior knowledge. Service composition is responsible for finding an alternative device when a sensor failed. To do so, we can prevent the running service from being interrupted.

Hot Hit Predictor, Final Project of Artificial Intelligent, 2010
This project was to predict hit songs by simply sampling song context. What we did was sampling Billboard Hot 100 songs and ones that have not made it to Hot 100.
